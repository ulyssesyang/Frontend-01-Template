## 字符串分析算法

### [字典树](trie.html)

- 大量字符串的完整模式匹配

### [KMP](KMP.html)

- Fast Pattern Matching in Strings (by Knuth-Morris-Pratt, SIAM, 1977)
- 长字符串中找子串，DNA pattern matching
- time complexity: O(M+N)
- Two Stages:
  - Pre-processing: table building O(M)
  - Matching: O(N)

- 长字符串中找子串升级版，时间复杂度O(M+N)
